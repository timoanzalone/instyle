IN STYLE has a unique vision embodied by two different aims: to build a relationship where the client is enhanced with trust and to maximize the quality of the distribution.

IN STYLE was established in 2005, and it all started with an independent showroom of 750 square meters at L’Isola District, close to the futuristic area of Corso Como. Due to a fast business growth, in 2011, IN STYLE GROUP moved to a bigger showroom of 1,700 square meters in via Palermo, in the heart of Brera Art District.

A SHOWROOM with 12 years of experience made IN STYLE leader in the market focusing on advanced contemporary brands and adding an “out-of-the-box”meaning to the new concept of fashion.

The result was that in 2011 IN STYLE GROUP expanded with DISTRIBUTION being able to provide brands with complete logistic service.
With great passion for fashion and expanding knowledge of territory, In Style partnered with Brama Sportswear to open Brama In Style. A new showroom located in Madrid, boasting the same exceptional brand selection, specializing in the territory of Spain.
